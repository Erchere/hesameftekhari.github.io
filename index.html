AOEIII: DE Mod

Made the ideas and coded The Uprising mod which is a mod that makes the revolutions from European civilizations viable for treaty by balancing and adjusting the revolution decks for each civilizations separately to bring new mechanics to the old civilizations and makes them up to date for late game team supremacy and treaty games. Initial version was released by Ropi (Revolution Extended) and afterward I made the coding for the mode by myself (The Uprising), created multiple new techs and cards with unique abilities for each revolt and civilizations. As an example one of the new tech which is enabled after sneding the Grazing card for India will enable the sacred cows to trickle export.

The Arena

One of the best ways for new players to learn the new counter units and the effiency of the units in terms of population and cost is to demonstrate how each unit perform against other units. So, I created The Arena series so this demonstration will be easier. Created scenarios with different background themes. On these scenarios, set specific trigers on the units to show how effective are each units against another in different ages and different techs.
