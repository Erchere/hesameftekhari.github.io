AOEIII: DE Mods and Maps

Made the ideas and design for two different maps which are Lakota Plain and Lakota River which are suitable for competetive treaty games. Generated and published by Vividlyplain. Made the ideas for Revolution Extended mod which is a mod that makes the revolutions from European civilizations viable for treaty by balancing and adjusting the revolution decks for each civilizations separately to bring new mechanics to the old civilizations and makes them up to date for treaty games. Generated and published by Ropi.

The Arena

One of the best ways for new players to learn the new counter units and the effiency of the units in terms of population and cost is to demonstrate how each unit perform against other units. So, I created The Arena series so this demonstration will be easier. Created scenarios with different background themse. On these scenarios, set specific trigers on the units to show how effective are each units against another in different ages.
